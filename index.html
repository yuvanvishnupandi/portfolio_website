Sethuraman Mangudi is an Indian cinematographer with over 36 years of experience and a respected member of the Western India Cinematographers Association (WICA). He has worked on feature films, ad films, and television across multiple languages in India.

He began his journey under the mentorship of Mani Ratnam and gained valuable experience with top cinematographers such as Kabir Lal, Santosh Sivan , and Johnny Lal. Some of his notable works include Krishna Cottage, Kaisay Kahein, Impatient Vivek, Cypher, Rashtraputra, and Andha Naal Nyabagam.

Known for his strong work ethic, discipline, and collaborative nature, he brings a high level of professionalism and dedication to every project.
